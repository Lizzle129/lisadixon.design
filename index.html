My Process

How I Work

Plan

Every project starts with a good plan. I enjoy researching the problem I am trying to solve and sketching up my ideas to share.

Build

Taking my ideas from sketches to protypes is always thrilling. It is verey exciting to see my designs come to life on the screen.

Test

I always participate in the UAT and QA process and enjoy getting feedback from the users. This allows me to go back and improve my work even more.

Ship

After prototyping, testing and revising a web application it is always exciting to sign off on the final solution and see it used in real life situations.
