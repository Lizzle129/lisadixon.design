My Approach

Workflow

Research / Discovery

I take the time to understand users—their needs, pain points, and behaviors—through interviews, surveys, competitive analysis, and
usability testing.

Ideation / Strategy

I translate research insights into actionable ideas, defining user flows, information architecture, and interaction strategies that align with business goals.

Design / Prototyping

I create wireframes, high-fidelity designs, and interactive prototypes that bring solutions to life both visually and functionally, iterating continuously based on feedback.

Testing / Iteration

validate designs with real users, gather feedback, analyze results, and refine products to ensure they are intuitive, effective, and aligned with both user needs and business objectives.
