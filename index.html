My Process

How I Work

Plan

Every project starts with a good plan. I enjoy researching the problem I am trying to solve and sketching up my ideas to share.

Build

Taking my ideas from sketches to protypes is always thrilling. It is verey exciting to see my designs come to life on the screen.

About Me

My Interests

Hi, I'm Lisa. Outside of being a Designer I have many other interests. I live in McHenry, IL in a beautiful Modern Century home on the Fox River just outside of the Chain of Lakes. I spend a lot of my time remodeling my home, hanging with my dog Abbey or out on the water enjoying the scenery. I enjoy cooking, listening to music and you can usually find me outside in our yard when the weather is nice.

Music

I love listening to music when I am doing anything. I listen to everything from Classic Rock to House Music. Music is a big part of my daily life and it helps me focus when I am working on projects.

Cooking

I love cooking and enjoying making my own new recipes. I grow my own herbs and vegetables in my garden. Cooking relaxes me and I enjoy sharing my food with friends and family.

Photography

Photography started out as a hobby for me but has become a big part of my everyday work. I enjoy taking original images to add an extra personal touch to all my projects.

Travel

I have been all over the world and every year I enjoy taking a trip to somewhere new. Exploring the world and experiencing different cultures is very important to me.
