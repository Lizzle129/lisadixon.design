Bass Pro Shops & Cabela's

Project Description

For the last year I have worked on Bass Pro Shops and Cabela's UX Design team. During that time we worked to give both websites a fresh new look and improve the customer experience. I worked closely with the Project Manager, Developers, QA and other designers to create a seemless user experience.

Project Details

Website UXUI Design


Research

Every update made to the websites had to be supported by research. We used many different online analytic tools to pull data on user behavior and activity. My research also included competitive analysis, user testing and online research.

UIUX Design

The design phase of the work was user centered while taking into account the different personas that interact with the websites. Many different designs were prototyped and tested before the final design was shared with Product Managers and Stakeholders.

Testing

Once the final designs were approved I worked closely with the Digital Product Manager and Development teams to get the websites updated. I helped the QA team test each release and wrote out tickets for any bugs or fixes that were found during that process.

Product Management

Once the final product has been released the work did not end. Managing the product and making sure it still operates and looks how it was designed was a part of the ongoing work for both websites.
